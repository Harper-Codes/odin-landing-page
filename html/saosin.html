Saosin

The High and Driving Band

Despite his full-time commitment to bands such as Circa Survive and L.S. Dunes, as well as dividing his time amongst part-time bands like Saosin and The Sound of Animals Fighting, since 2008 Anthony has maintained a successfull solo act, which is currently referred to as The High and Driving Band. With support from various musicians, many of them part of their own bands, Anthony has released five full length albums and has led numerous tours promoting these albums and beyond
